Welcome to the best books application!



This is an application where you can browse through
a collection of well-known books. You will be able to
see the title of the book, the author and the year it was
published, among other data.

There are some classical titles in this collection,
with many of them being best-sellers and very well known
among readers. Each book in this collection will have a
set of reviews about it that have been made by past readers.
